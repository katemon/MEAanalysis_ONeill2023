The second option allows you to encircle a group of dots by clicking the vertices of a polygon. The waveforms of this group will be displayed in a new figure, again giving you some options.

 

 
Once you have selected a spike population the 'sort' button will assign a clusternumber and a subpopulation name to them. See the messages in the command window for the values assigned.

Please check the specific help functions for further details and have a look at the tooltips associated with some of the functions.
