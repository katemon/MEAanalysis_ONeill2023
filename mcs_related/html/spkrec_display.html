Spike sorting  - reviewing spike waveforms vs. time
Back to overview

 
This display is intended to review the cluster assignments in the context of the spike train, and compare the waveforms in a time slice of you record. 
 
 
You may 
  • select a time window (when opening the tool or via the options menu), 
  • show overlays of the spike cutouts in that window, 
  • zoom in on a particular region of the window. The l, r ,u , and d keys on your keyboard will move the zoom window to the left, right, up, and down respectively. 
  • 'Reset zoom' will return you to the full window.
